Hello, we are Vermaak, a media company based in Africa.

We collaborate with creative talents and other production & publication companies across the continent to produce high-quality content that is recognized globally. Our mission: "To empower, connect, and promote African creatives in the industry, while also digitizing and promoting African culture through our work."

We offer a wide array of service and products with an African niche. See some of our works or get in touch.

Services

Our offerings are spread across several branches of our business, including Vermaak Production, Vermaak Books, Vermaak Digital, Vermaak Events, and Vermaak Games. Each branch offers unique support for creatives and corporates alike, aimed at cultivating an innovative and interactive creative industry and empowering change across Africa.

Vermaak Productions

Our production branch offers high-quality photography, videography, film production, screenwriting, and everything cinematography. Our team consists of experienced and talented professionals who specialize in creating stunning visuals and producing captivating stories.

Vermaak Books

Here at Vermaak Books, we help authors across the African continent bring their stories to life. Our services include book publishing, blog series, book reading events and much more. We believe in promoting cultural authenticity and African stories to the world.

Vermaak Digital

Our digital branch specializes in developing cutting-edge, online experiences for companies. Our expertise covers creating digital brand identities, company profiles and much more. We help companies build their digital identity using a blend of creativity and innovation.

Vermaak Events

Here, we specialize in creating customized, online experiences for Corporate Events through a well-designed and developed platform. Be it product launches, company conferences, or a virtual expo, we bring real interactions to digital space. Our team leverages the latest technology and expertise to deliver exceptional digital events regardless of the attendees' location.

Vermaak Games

At Vermaak Games, we are passionate about creating dynamic and engaging video games that showcase Africa's rich cultural history. We develop games that showcase African cultural icons and mythical creatures, while also providing popular games commonly played by Africans. Our games combine entertainment with education.

About Us

Who we are.

Vermaak is a media company based in Africa, offering a wide array of service and products with an African niche. We collaborate with creative talents and other production & publication companies across the continent to produce high-quality content that is recognized globally.
Our mission: "To empower, connect, and promote African creatives in the industry, while also digitizing and promoting African culture through our work."
